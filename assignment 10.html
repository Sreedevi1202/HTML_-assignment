Special Characters and HTML entities

HTML entities are used to display special characters

Here are some examples:

  • &(Ampersand)
  • <(less than)
  • >(greater than)
  • ©(copyright)
  • ®(registered)
  • ™(Trademark)

In HTML ,you can use these entities to include characters in your content without causing issues.
